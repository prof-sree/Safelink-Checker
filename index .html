Actionable Tips

Common Indian Scams

  • Fake UPI/Refund: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. PIN is only for sending.
  • Impersonation: If a family member urgently asks for money via unknown number, verify by calling their old number.
  • OTP Scams: Never share an OTP for a "delivery" you didn't order.
  • Job/Investment Links: Part-time YouTube/Telegram job offers promising high returns are 100% scams.
